My Reviews

Sunbeam Coffee

I visited: August 3rd 2020

My beverage was: Cold Brew

The Atmosphere was: Rustic

Overall Rating: Pretty Good!

Personal thoughts: Spacious, comfy atmosphere with lots of natural light. I was surprised with live music - that I actually liked! Pleasantly surprised overall. I will be back!

Sacred Grounds

I visited: October 15th 2020

My beverage was: Hot Tea

The Atmosphere was: Artistic, Quiet

Overall Rating: Ehhh

Personal thoughts: One of the best places for vegan food in the city. There was local art on the walls and they carried a local roast that I honestly didn't like very much. Another thing was how uncomfortable the chairs and tables are. I'd go back, but only on a nice day when I can sit outside.

BluePrint Coffee

I visited: December 1st 2020

My beverage was: Latte

The Atmosphere was: Industrial, Busy, Loud

Overall Rating: Not Completely Horrible

Personal thoughts: Like the food and the quality of the coffee. When it is crowded, the ambient noise is pervasive and distracting. Difficult to concentrate in that type of a setting. Still like the place.

The Mud House

I visited: January 19th 2021

My beverage was: Latte

The Atmosphere was: Cottage-Core, Artistic

Overall Rating: Amazing!

Personal Thoughts: Make sure to go upstairs, it's gorgeously furnished and well lit.. There are many places to work including a group table, armchairs, a window facing bar workspace, and a few small tables!

The Crave House

I visited: January 31st 2021

My beverage was: Iced Coffee

The Atmosphere was: Chic, Industrial

Overall Rating: Amazing!

Personal Thoughts: Had the best caffeine rush of my life. They use a Chemex glass brewer which takes everthing to the next level.

Park Avenue Coffee

I visited: January 31st 2021

My beverage was: French Press/Pour-Over

The Atmosphere was: Cozy, Artistic

Overall Rating: Pretty Good!

Personal Thoughts: Really fun location right in a quiet little neighborhood. Great for walking the dog and grabbing some coffee.
